Charles Fourier

19th Century French Socialist Thinker


Some Facts about Fourier

  • French philosopher
  • Coined the term f'eminisme
  • Advocated concern and cooperation as the means to create social harmony
  • Considered poverty to be the main cause of society's problems
  • Said the actions of men are governed by the passions
  • Proposed the establishment of self-contained communites called phalanxes where
    work would be organized to make the best use of passions
